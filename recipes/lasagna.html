Lasagna

Meat and Cheese

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is made with layers of flat noodles, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is a popular dish in Italian cuisine and is often served as a main course.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
